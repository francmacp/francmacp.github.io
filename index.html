History of the iPhone: 13 Years of Progress

iPhone. Say that word anywhere in thw world and it will be immediately recognized as the phone withe apple on the back. This did not happen by accident. It all started in California back in 2007 when Steve Jobs announced the original Apple iPhone. And the rest was history. Keep reading for a brief history of the iPhone and the changes that have made it the phone it is today.

Below are three key changes that have made the iPhone what it is today

  • Initial release in 2007
  • Siri is introduced in 2011
  • 2014, Apple introduces the first two iPhones with different screen sizes, an update that would be repeated annually to this day.

These are just a few examples of updates to the iPhone over the years. Read the iPhone Wikipedia Page for a more in depth understanding of the history of the Apple iPhone and what changes have been made to it over the years.
